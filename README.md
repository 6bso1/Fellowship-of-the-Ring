## General Info
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The application is an application designed for astroturf enthusiasts. You can reach the carpet pitches through the application, you can see the working hours, telephone and comments about the carpet pitch. At the same time, if there are missing players in your team, you can contact the players suitable for your team on the team search page, or you can contact the teams that are looking for players for their team if you want to play a match but do not have a team. In addition, if you do not have an opponent to play with, you can make pleasant matches by contacting teams looking for opponents in the opponent finding section.

## Features
* Sign up
* Search Player
* Search Team
* Search For Opposing Team
* Search Astroturf
* Evaluate Astroturf
* Send a Message to a User of Your Choice
* Add Your Favorite Astroturf to Favourites
* Edit Profile
